Services We Provide at Hicon

At Hicon, we offer a range of professional salon services to help you look and feel your best. Our expert team is dedicated to providing personalized care with top-notch products and techniques.

  • Haircut

    Get the perfect haircut that suits your style and personality. Our experienced stylists will work with you to create a look you’ll love and enjoy.

  • Beard Setting

    Keep your beard in perfect shape with our expert beard setting services. Whether you want a fresh trim or a complete styling, our barbers will give you the look you desire.

  • Hair Color

    Change up your look with a new hair color. From vibrant shades to subtle tones, our colorists use high-quality dyes to give you a customized color.

  • Massage

    Relax and unwind with our rejuvenating head massage. Perfect for relieving stress and promoting circulation, this treatment will leave you feeling refreshed and relaxed.

  • Face Treatments

    Pamper your skin with our luxurious facial treatments. From deep cleansing to anti-aging therapies, we offer a range of facials designed to restore your skin’s natural glow.

  • Hair Wash

    Treat your hair to a refreshing wash using premium products. Our hair wash service includes a gentle cleanse, scalp massage, and conditioning treatment for healthy hair.
